    To our patient friends... encourage your dental team to move in this direction. To our oral systemic colleagues... your patients are living longer!

    'Houston... we have a solution!'

  • BLOG: 11-29-17
  • Vascular health improved by oral health

    An eight year study of almost 600 high-risk patients was conducted to see if cardiovascular disease outcomes could be improved.

    The study participants had to take a simple salivary test to find high-risk pathogens. Just like the one seen here. If they had any of these 'bad bugs' ... they were targeted and eliminated as part of the study protocol.

    The participants were also asked to make other lifestyle changes.

    When oral bacteria were 'killed'... they found vascular health improved very quickly.

    The pathogens in the mouth were found living along artery walls and doing damage.

    At the end of 8 years, there were significantly less cardiovascular events than a 'norm' population and vascular health was greatly enhanced when viewed by CMIT imaging.

    The standard of care for all at risk patients: a simple salivary test by a trained dental team. To our patient friends- ask your dental team for this test if you are concerned about your heart health. To our oral systemic colleagues- your patients are blessed!

    Spread the word friends, heart attacks have been the #1 killer of Americans for 100 years. Dentists have been cleaning teeth about the same way for the same 100 years... its time for a new standard!
