Oral systemic education- The process of identifying high-risk pathogens and high-risk patients all while understanding the inflammatory and genetic links connecting them to chronic disease. A new standard for dental education based on current and relevant evidence. Medical histories will take on new meaning, therapy targeted to both local biomes and systemic micro-biomes, antibiotic stewardship principles may surprise you, and the business model will grow the practice.


Many dental teams know oral health is connected to systemic health but only a few have implemented clinical practices into dental hygiene that will change chronic disease outcomes for patients.
